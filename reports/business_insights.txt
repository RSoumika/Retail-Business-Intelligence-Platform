RETAIL BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Retail Business Intelligence Platform project analyzes 9,994 retail transactions between 2014 and 2017. The objective is to identify revenue drivers, profitability trends, regional performance, customer behavior, and product-level opportunities. Analysis was conducted using MySQL, with findings intended to support data-driven business decision-making.

1. Overall Business Performance
- Total Revenue: 2,272,450.07
- Total Profit: 282,858.46
- Total Orders: 4,931
- Overall Profit Margin: 12.45%

2. Regional Analysis
- West Region generated the highest revenue (713,471.45) and highest profit (106,021.47).
- East Region was the second-best performing region.
- South Region generated the lowest revenue (388,983.59).
- Central Region generated lower profit than South despite having higher revenue, indicating possible discounting or cost-related issues.


3. Category Analysis
- Technology is the best-performing category.
- Technology generated the highest revenue (835,900.14) and the highest profit (145,387.81).
- Office Supplies generated lower revenue than Furniture but significantly higher profit.
- Furniture generated strong revenue (733,047.06) but very low profit (16,980.72).
- Furniture appears to be the least profitable category and requires further investigation.

4. Customer Analysis
- Sean Miller generated the highest sales revenue (25,043.07) but resulted in a loss of 1,980.75.
- Tamara Chand is one of the most valuable customers, generating both high revenue and high profit.
- Several customers contribute significantly to overall revenue, indicating the importance of customer retention strategies.
- High-revenue customers should be evaluated not only on sales but also on profitability.

5. Product Analysis
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier is the highest-performing product with revenue of 61,599.83 and profit of 25,199.94.
- Several high-revenue products generate losses, including:
  - Cisco TelePresence System EX90 Videoconferencing
  - GBC DocuBind P400 Electric Binding System
  - High Speed Automatic Electric Letter Opener
- Product performance should be evaluated using both revenue and profitability metrics.
- Loss-making products require investigation into pricing, discounts, and operational costs.
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier is not only the highest revenue-generating product but also the most profitable product, generating 25,199.94 in profit.

6. Yearly Sales Analysis

- The business shows strong overall growth from 2014 to 2017.
- Revenue increased from 481,763.83 in 2014 to 724,994.51 in 2017.
- Profit increased from 49,044.48 in 2014 to 92,775.45 in 2017.
- 2017 was the best-performing year in both revenue and profit.
- Revenue slightly declined in 2015 compared to 2014, but profit increased significantly, indicating improved profitability.
- The company achieved sustained growth between 2014 and 2017, with revenue increasing by over 50% and profit nearly doubling during the period.


7. Monthly Sales Analysis

- November was the highest-performing month, generating revenue of 348,834.58.
- December was the second-highest performing month with revenue of 321,161.04.
- September also demonstrated strong sales performance, generating 303,536.69 in revenue.
- February was the weakest month, generating only 59,012.82 in revenue.
- January also recorded relatively low sales compared to other months.
- Sales activity increases significantly during the final quarter of the year (October–December).
- The business exhibits clear seasonal patterns, with revenue peaking during year-end months.
- Marketing campaigns, inventory planning, and promotional activities should be prioritized during the fourth quarter to maximize revenue opportunities.

8. State-wise Analysis

- California is the highest-performing state, generating revenue of 450,567.63 and profit of 74,669.41.
- New York is the second most profitable state with revenue of 309,453.56 and profit of 73,507.20.
- Texas generated high revenue (169,553.58) but incurred a loss of 25,534.94.
- Pennsylvania generated revenue of 114,911.32 but resulted in a loss of 15,446.46.
- Florida, Illinois, and Ohio also reported negative profitability despite substantial sales.
- High revenue does not always translate into high profitability.
- States with negative profits should be investigated for excessive discounts, operational inefficiencies, or unfavorable product mixes.

9. Discount Impact Analysis

- Discount levels have a significant impact on profitability.
- Transactions with no discount generated an average profit of 68.11.
- A 10% discount still maintained healthy profitability with an average profit of 96.06.
- Profitability declines substantially as discount levels increase.
- Discounts of 30% or higher consistently resulted in negative average profits.
- The highest losses occurred at the 50% discount level, where average profit fell to -310.70 per transaction.
- The analysis suggests that aggressive discounting is a major contributor to profit erosion.
- The company should review its discount strategy and establish thresholds to prevent loss-making sales.

10. Recommendations
- Investigate Central Region profitability.
- Analyze successful products in West Region and replicate strategies in other regions.
- Focus on increasing sales in South Region while maintaining profitability.

11. Data Quality Assessment

- The dataset contains 9,994 records and 21 attributes.
- No missing values were identified across any column.
- No duplicate records were found.
- Data types were validated using Pandas.
- Numerical fields such as Sales, Quantity, Discount, and Profit were correctly stored as numeric data types.
- Date fields (Order Date and Ship Date) were identified as text and subsequently converted to datetime format for time-series analysis.
- The dataset demonstrated high data quality and required minimal preprocessing.
